## XoopsFormButton

#### Definition
```php
XoopsFormButton(str $caption, str $name, bool $value="", button|submit|reset $type="button")
```

#### Usage
```php
$xoopsForm->addElement(new XoopsFormButton('Button Caption', 'button_id', 'Button-Text', 'submit'));
```
